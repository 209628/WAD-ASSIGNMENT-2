WEB 1.0

Definition:

- It is the first generation of the World Wide Web that existed from approximately 1991 to 2004.

- It is often referred to as the "Read Only Web" because users could only read and view content on websites but could not interact with or contribute to it.

- Web 1.0 marked the beginning of the internet era and laid the foundation for all future developments in computer and information technology.

Characteristics of Web 1.0:

  1. Has static HTML pages
  2. No interaction
  3. Users can only consume content
  4. Navigation was simple and websites were essentially digital versions of printed documents or brochures.

* Has sites like Yahoo and GeoCities.
