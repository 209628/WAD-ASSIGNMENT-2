INTRANET

Definition:

- A private internal network that is accessible to members of an organization such as a company,school or government institution.

- It consists of web pages and resources that are only available inside the organization.

- Intranets host internal portals, HR systems, document repositories and communication tools.


How it works:

It works by connecting all the computers and devices within an organization through a private network. It uses the same protocols as the internet such as HTTP, FTP and TCP/IP but operates within a closed and secure environment. Employees can access the intranet by logging in with their authorized credentials either from within the office or remotely through a Virtual Private Network (VPN). The intranet is protected by firewalls and security systems that prevent unauthorized users from gaining access.
